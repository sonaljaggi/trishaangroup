Overview

Our Mathematics and Science Coaching Consultancy provides tailored academic support for learners across Key Stages 2–5, including GCSEs, A-Levels, and university foundation courses. We specialise in enhancing subject understanding, improving examination performance, and fostering long-term confidence in STEM subjects.

Our Approach

We adopt a personalised, evidence-based methodology aligned with the UK National Curriculum and major examination boards (AQA, Edexcel, OCR, WJEC). Our consultancy evaluates individual learning needs and designs structured programmes that promote both conceptual clarity and practical problem-solving.

Services Offered

Individual Coaching

  • One-to-one tutoring in Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and Biology
  • Support in core topics: algebra, mechanics, organic chemistry, cellular biology
  • Regular progress monitoring and performance reports

Group Sessions

  • Small, focused workshops for GCSE and A-Level exam preparation
  • Revision masterclasses covering key topics
  • Exam technique and past paper analysis

Academic Consulting

  • Curriculum planning and enhancement for schools
  • Support for intervention programmes
  • Training for teaching staff in effective STEM pedagogy

University Preparation

  • Foundation‐level Mathematics and Science coaching
  • Guidance for admissions tests (STEP, MAT, BMAT)
  • Interview preparation and personal statement support
